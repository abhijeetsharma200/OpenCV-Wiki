h1. Why citing OpenCV

If you are using OpenCV in your research-related documents, it is recommended that you cite OpenCV. This way, other researchers can better understand your proposed-method. Your method is more reproducible and thus gaining better credibility.


h1. How to cite OpenCV

Below is the BibTex entry for citing OpenCV

<pre><code>@article{opencv_library,
author = {Bradski, G.},
citeulike-article-id = {2236121},
journal = {Dr. Dobb's Journal of Software Tools},
keywords = {bibtex-import},
posted-at = {2008-01-15 19:21:54},
priority = {4},
title = {{The OpenCV Library}},
year = {2000}
}
</code></pre>
